General

Bookmark Suggestions

The extension scans your browsing history and suggests pages worth bookmarking based on how often you visit them.

A page must be visited at least this many times within the lookback window to appear as a bookmark suggestion. Default: 5.

How far back in your history to look for frequently visited pages. Default: 14 days.

Low-Use Tab Detection

Identifies open tabs you rarely visit so you can bookmark or close them to reduce clutter.

How far back to check visit history when deciding if a tab is low-use. Default: 30 months.

Tabs visited this many times or fewer within the lookback window are flagged as low-use. Default: 2.

Data & Privacy

How long the extension keeps internal tab activity records. Older records are pruned automatically. Default: 30 days.
